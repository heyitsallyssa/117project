Instagram Contest Rules

The Thankful For Instagram contest will be held from November 21-25, 2016. No purchase necessary. Just share the photo on a public Instagram account and tag @The117ProjectCA. You must include a caption of what you are thankful for along with our Instagram handle and #ThankfulFor117. A winner will be chosen at random and announced on Sunday, November 27, 2016 via our Instagram account.


The winner will be awarded (1) custom shirt order from our Etsy shop. Your artwork must be your own and not infringe on any copyrights or protected works. You can read more on what is included in a custom order on our storefront. The winning custom order includes no more than two ink colors on one shirt. The 117 Project must approve the design prior to printing.

Good luck!
